About Us

Indiranagar Sangeetha Sabha is situated in Katreguppi, Bangalore. Sabha was founded in early 2012, has actively engaged itself in the promotion of classical carnatic music. A registered trust authored by Vid. Bangalore Narendra, a leading mridangam artist and a band of dedicated trustees, the Sabha currently has over 10 members. For more than a decade, the Sabha has been instrumental in fulfilling the cultural aspirations in the southern part of Bangalore city.
